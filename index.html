The Game

Wand Wars is a 2D local arena bloodsport currently in development.

Partake in the superhip event where Wizlords and Wizladies duke it out in magickal combat not dissimilar to our own court-based sports. The skillz and fortitude demonstrated by participants inspire demons and angels alike. The pain is temporary, the glory — eternal.
